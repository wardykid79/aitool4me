What is AI?

AI is software that can understand, write, summarise, create, analyse, plan, and automate tasks. It can help with emails, documents, customer messages, images, websites, business admin, research, marketing, and more.

You don't need to be technical to use AI tools. Most work by you simply typing what you need — just like sending a message — and the AI responds with something useful.

⚠️
AI is powerful, but not always right.

Don't trust it with legal, medical, tax, or financial decisions without human review. Treat AI as a helpful assistant, not an expert.
